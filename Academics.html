Academics

My SMART Goal

My SMART goal was to get all my work done in a timely manner. This goal was kind of split into two parts for me: working alone and working with peers. To achieve this goal I have done my best to stay on track while working with peers and found music that helps me concentrate while working on homework. Going forward in the year with my goal, I will focus on concentrating on getting classwork and homework done in a timely manner. I can't do much more to work on working with peers to not get distracted, so working alone will be my purpose.

My Stretch

My stretch this year was working with peers and friends without getting distracted, which is closely tied to my SMART goal. I think my stretch remains to be a stretch but has gotten better from the beginning of the year. Staying on task while working with peers is still hard for me but working with friends is easier to do without all of us getting distracted.

My Strength

My strength was staying on top of classwork and I think this strength has remained constant for me. I missed a lot of school going through my concussion in addition to other sicknesses I have had and I think staying on top of classwork helped me not get too behind.
